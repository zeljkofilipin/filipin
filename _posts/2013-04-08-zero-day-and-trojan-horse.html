I have heard about Zero Day and Trojan Horse novels by Mark Russinovich at Security Now podcast. Steve has recommended the book several times. Mark appeared as a guest at episode 370 of the podcast.

I have "gently" mentioned to my wife that I would like to read the books, so I got them as a present. I have read both books and I have really liked them. I will not go into a lot of detail, but both books talk about what could happen if somebody really wanted to take advantage of problems in contemporary software. I would recommend both books. On the podcast, Mark mentioned he is writing the third book. I am looking forward to reading it.
