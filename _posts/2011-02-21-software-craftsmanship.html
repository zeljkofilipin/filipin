I have read Software Craftsmanship: The New Imperative by Pete McBreen. I liked the book and I want to write this blog post as a reminder for myself that I have read it. Unfortunately, I do not have the time to write a good blog post, so here is just one thought:

Software engineering works on really big projects, software craftsmanship works on smaller projects.

I like the idea of moving from apprentice to journeymen to master.
