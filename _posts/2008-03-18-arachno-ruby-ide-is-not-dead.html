This morning I was pleasantly surprised when I found a piece of e-mail in my inbox from Lothar Scholz, developer of Arachno Ruby IDE saying that Arachno is not dead. I have been using it for years, but recently I switched to NetBeans because Arachno really looked dead. I will give Arachno another try.
