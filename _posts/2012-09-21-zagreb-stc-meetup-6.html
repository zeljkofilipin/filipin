Zagreb STC MeetUp #6

Yesterday I was at Zagreb STC MeetUp #6. As always, it was organized by Karlo Å mid at Ericsson Nikola Tesla.

Another great meetup. As usual, there were about 10 of us. Most of us were testers but there were a few developers too. We started with introductions. Everybody said a few words about themselves, what they do in life and what they were testing in the last week. We thought that would take 10-20 minutes, but the introductions were frequently interrupted with a lot of questions, so it lasted for about 90 minutes.

The pizza came about that time so we had a short break. After the break Manuel Vidonis gave an introduction to software testing talk (pdf, slides, both in Croatian). His talk was also frequently interrupted with a lot of questions, so it lasted until the end of the meetup. In the last few minutes Manuel talked about Traffic Manager, Windows Phone game that he is developing.

I brought the books that I own and our little library continues! (I am still reading Testing Computer Software (2nd Edition) by Cem Kaner, Jack Falk and Hung Q. Nguyen.)

Of course there were pizza and soft drinks, thanks to Ericsson Nikola Tesla.
